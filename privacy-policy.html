Privacy Policy

How information is collected, used, and protected when you visit iarslanaly.dev or contact Arslan Ali.

Last updated: June 24, 2026
Plain-language summary

Analytics is optional and does not load unless you accept it. Information submitted through the contact form is used only to respond to your enquiry and discuss potential work.

1. Who is responsible for this website?

This website is operated by Arslan Ali, a freelance Flutter developer based in Lahore, Pakistan. Privacy questions can be sent to contact@iarslanaly.dev.

2. Information you provide

When you use the contact form, you may provide your name, email address, phone number, subject, message, and information about your project. Name, email, subject, and message are required. The phone number is optional.

This information is used to respond to your enquiry, understand your project, prepare an estimate, and communicate about potential services. Please do not submit sensitive personal, financial, medical, or confidential information through the form.

3. Contact form processing

Contact form submissions are transmitted using EmailJS. EmailJS processes the submitted information to deliver the message by email. Its handling of information is also governed by its own privacy terms.

Messages may be retained in email records for as long as reasonably necessary to respond, maintain business records, resolve disputes, or meet legal obligations. You may request deletion where applicable.

4. Optional analytics and cookies

This website uses Google Analytics with measurement ID G-0NYCV99TDH only after you select “Accept analytics.” Analytics may collect information such as pages viewed, approximate location, device and browser details, referral source, interactions, and visit duration.

Google Analytics may use cookies or similar browser storage. IP anonymization is requested in the website configuration. Analytics information is used to understand aggregate website performance and improve content and usability, not to sell personal information.

If you decline, Google Analytics is not loaded. Your consent choice is stored in your browser’s local storage so the website can remember it. You can reopen the banner through “Cookie settings” in the footer.

5. Legal basis and consent

Where consent is required, optional analytics is processed on the basis of your consent. Contact information is processed to respond to your request and take steps toward a possible service agreement. Certain information may also be retained where there is a legitimate business need or legal obligation.

6. Third-party services

This website may use or link to services including Google Analytics, EmailJS, Google Fonts, Upwork, LinkedIn, GitHub, and social networks. Visiting an external website is subject to that provider’s own privacy policy and terms. External links do not mean that this website controls those services.

7. Data sharing

Personal information is not sold. Information may be shared with service providers only where needed to operate the website, deliver contact messages, provide requested services, protect legal rights, or comply with law.

8. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect information. However, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

9. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or a copy of personal information. You may also withdraw analytics consent at any time through the footer’s cookie settings. Requests can be sent to contact@iarslanaly.dev.

10. Children’s privacy

This portfolio and its services are not directed to children, and personal information from children is not knowingly collected.

11. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the website, services, or legal requirements change. The latest revision date will appear at the top of this page.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email contact@iarslanaly.dev.
